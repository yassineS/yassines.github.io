## Introduction
In the field of bioinformatics and research, in general, productivity tools, backup strategies, software/code development and distribution, collaboration practices aren't standardized nor widespread. As a result we observe that collaboration is hard within the lab and across labs, also when it comes to code, very few host their code on GitHub or other public repositories, and less using version control tools (git/mercurial).
Here in this post I am trying to list a set of tools and best practices to make the life of anyone starting in the field to have a startup kit and a guide to make your life easier and save you time.
