## Introduction
In the field of bioinformatics and research in general, productivity tools, backup strategies, softwar/code developement and distribuition, collaboration practices aren't standardized nor widespread. As a result we observe that collaboration is hard within lab and accross labs, also when it comes to code, very few host their code on github or other public repository, and less using version control tools (git/mercurial).
Here in this post I am trying to list a set of tools and best practices to make the life of anyone starting in the field to have a startup-kit and a guide to make your life easier and save you time.
