I provide advisory and consulting services to government agencies and research bodies on applied genomics, bioinformatics, and cloud-backed analysis pipelines. Engagements typically combine technical design, capability assessment, and translation between research methodology and operational deployment.

For evidence of broader research uptake — including media reach, policy citations, and tier-1 outlet coverage — see [Research Impact](/about/impact).

## Centre for Invasive Species Solutions (CISS)

**Pilot project: gold-standard methodology for dingo ancestry testing** — consultancy agreement, 2025.

A research-and-development engagement: systematically benchmark existing ancestry-testing approaches and develop a defensible, cost-effective standard suitable for adoption across Australian wildlife-management agencies. The deliverables sit at the level of *which method should jurisdictions be using, and on what evidence* — methodological rather than operational.

## Department of Primary Industries and Regions, South Australia (PIRSA)

**Dingo ancestry and admixture testing** — services agreement with the Minister for Primary Industries and Regional Development (acting through PIRSA), 2026.

A short, operationally focused service-delivery engagement supporting PIRSA's Biosecurity team: take the supplied tissue samples and return ancestry and admixture-proportion estimates using best-available methodology. Where the CISS work is *what test should we use*, this engagement is *test these samples, well*.
