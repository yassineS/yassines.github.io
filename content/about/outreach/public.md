## The Conversation

I write occasional explainers for [The Conversation](https://theconversation.com/profiles/yassine-souilmi-1243323/articles), covering recent papers in plain language for a general audience.

- *[What were dingoes like before the European invasion? Centuries-old DNA reveals a surprising history](https://theconversation.com/what-were-dingoes-like-before-the-european-invasion-centuries-old-dna-reveals-a-surprising-history-232491)* — 10 July 2024 (with Gabriel Conroy, Jane Balme, Sally Wasef).
- *[Ancient humans may have paused in Arabia for 30,000 years on their way out of Africa](https://theconversation.com/ancient-humans-may-have-paused-in-arabia-for-30-000-years-on-their-way-out-of-africa-206200)* — 24 May 2023 (with Ray Tobler, Shane T. Grey).
- *[Ancient DNA reveals a hidden history of human adaptation](https://theconversation.com/ancient-dna-reveals-a-hidden-history-of-human-adaptation-193251)* — 31 October 2022 (with Christian Huber, Ray Tobler).
- *[We found traces of humanity's age-old arms race with coronaviruses written in our DNA](https://theconversation.com/we-found-traces-of-humanitys-age-old-arms-race-with-coronaviruses-written-in-our-dna-163254)* — 24 June 2021 (with Ray Tobler).
