I'm excited to release a new way to discover and find new restaurants in Adelaide. We are so lucky here to have so many greate foody spots. However, that makes choosing one difficult sometimes.

<!--more-->

{{< image-text-wrap src="SCR-20260310-pdig.png" alt="Adelaide Open Food Map interactive restaurant map screenshot" width="250px" gap="30px" link="https://www.ysouilmi.com/adelaide_open_food_map/" target="_blank" >}}

That's why leverage the amzing project by Lauren Leek, with some modifications and little improvements to build a a data-driven restaurant discovery for Adelaide and immediate surrounding suburbs <a href="https://www.ysouilmi.com/adelaide_open_food_map/" target="_blank" rel="noopener"><strong>Adelaide Open Food Map</strong></a>.

The project collects restaurant data (e.g. via Google Maps API), runs a simple ML model to surface "underrated" spots by comparing ratings to review counts, and generates interactive HTML maps with cuisine filters and borough-style analysis.
